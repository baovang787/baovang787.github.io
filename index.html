How to get IV Hydration at Home

Appointment

Request an appointment using our online portal or call/text: 651-236-7759. Appointments are based on geographic location & nurse availability throughout the Twin Cities areas.

Medical history review

Your medical history will be reviewed by our staff to ensure that IV treatment is safe and appropriate. Our medical staff will help you tailor your treatment needs during this process. 

Service

Appointment last approximately 1.5-2.5 hours depending on your body and are conducted by Registered Nurses (RNs) that are licensed & insured in the State of Minnesota.

The RN who administered the IV will follow up with the patient to ensure that no unintended side effects are developing after the treatment. 

Frequently Asked Questions

01. Is IV therapy safe?

Yes, IV therapy is safe when administered by our certified professionals, following a thorough health assessment to ensure compatibility with your health status.

02. How long does an IV therapy session last?

typically, sessions last between 45 and 90 minutes, depending on your specific treatment plan and your body’s response.

03. Will I experience any side effects after the treatment?

Most clients face no issues; however, minor side effects can include bruising at the injection site or a cooling sensation during the infusion.

04. How soon will I notice the effects of the IV therapy?

Many clients feel an immediate improvement in their overall well-being, but individual experiences with the onset of effects may vary.

05. How frequently should I come in for IV therapy?

The frequency varies per individual needs and goals, which we’ll determine together during your initial consultation and ongoing assessments..

06. What payment methods do you accept? 

We accept a variety of payment methods to make it convenient for you. We accept cash, debit card, credit card, and HSA.
